	•	Cultural caption

	•	Like button
	•	Comment button
	•	Share button

Key Features:

✔ Auto-fetch more posts
✔ Double-tap to like
✔ Subtle heart animation
✔ Light haptic feedback

	•	Music
	•	Art
	•	Lifestyle

Clean and minimal.
